# Privacy Policy

HeaderShim stores the header rules you create, including any values you enter, in your browser's local storage (chrome.storage.local) on your device. A value you type can itself be sensitive, such as an Authorization token or a Cookie value, because setting those headers is what the extension does.

HeaderShim does not transmit, collect, sell, or share this or any other data. It has no servers, no accounts, no analytics, and no remotely hosted code, and its content security policy blocks it from making network requests. Removing the extension deletes its stored data.
